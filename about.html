About JusticeBot

JusticeBot is an AI-powered legal assistant designed to simplify Indian law interpretation and awareness for citizens. It uses natural language processing (NLP) and OpenAI’s advanced models to provide concise and reliable information about various legal sections.

Key Legal Areas Covered:

  • ⚖️ Criminal Law: IPC Sections like 302, 420, 354, etc.
  • 💻 Cyber Law: IT Act, Section 66 for hacking and online crimes.
  • 👩‍👧 Family Law: Marriage, divorce, domestic violence, and dowry laws.
  • 📜 Civil Law: Property disputes and defamation cases.

Vision:

To bridge the gap between citizens and the justice system by making legal information accessible, easy to understand, and available anytime.
